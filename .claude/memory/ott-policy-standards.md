Source PDFs: `C:\Users\grosh\Dropbox\Overland Tailor 3rd Party Calibrators\Reference\Policy Memos\` (owner-authorized to read/retain, 2026-07-10). Confidential — repo is private. Approved by John F. Gobbels (OTT). Summary of each policy's binding rules:

- **0001 Tipping** — Never request a tip (no verbal ask, no tip field on payment forms). May accept a freely-offered tip.
- **0002 Receipts** — Every retail client gets a paid invoice/receipt (physical or electronic); provide without delay when requested.
- **0003 Logo Use & Merchandise** — OTT logo is protected; no reproduction/modification without written permission. Allowed w/o permission: tune-meet ads, your professional business website, vinyl stickers on a business vehicle. OTT shop = sole merch source; no resale of OTT merch.
- **0004 Selective Service Obligation** — Emissions-intact requirement. Do NOT tune vehicles missing any Federally Mandated Emissions Component (cats, O2/exhaust sensors), with defeat devices, active DTCs, unsafe condition, or questionable parts (aftermarket coil packs, "performance" MAF sensors). Matches [[brand-rules-locked]] emissions-intact positioning.
- **0005 Online Brand Presence** — Maintain a robust professional web presence: product info, services (platforms, forced induction), **visible up-front pricing before booking**, contact info. Min = independent website + Facebook. Professional decorum online. (tunedyota.com already complies.)
- **0006 Tune Transfer / Continued Support** — OTT support is tied to the owner at time of calibration. Same owner + proof of original cal → all updates + throttle adjustments. Vehicle sold to a new owner → support/updates CEASE until the new owner buys a calibration. OTT does not maintain sales records — that's the customer's/retailer's responsibility.
- **0007 Submission of Commissions (v1.1)** — **Commission + sales documentation due IN FULL by the 7th of the following month** (Jan due Feb 7). This is the authoritative deadline (supersedes 0012's "10th"). Discrepancies settled within 1 business day. This is WHY Tuned Yota's deadline is the 7th (reminder on the 5th) — it's policy, not just preference. See [[monthly-ott-calibration-report]].
- **0008 Pricing Adherence** — Must follow the OTT Pricing Sheet: MSRP **and** commission. Commission rates non-negotiable unless OTT initiates. Retail price changes in a market need OTT clearance first. Our commission engine prices the **commission** (what we owe OTT) straight from the April 2026 sheet — unaffected by our retail price. ⚠ **Tuned Yota's retail-price adaptations are OWNER/OTT-APPROVED (owner confirmed 2026-07-10) — DO NOT auto-align our retail prices to the sheet.** Commission owed is always the sheet value (or owner override); our customer-facing retail price is our own approved number. A pricing-adherence deviation report (our retail vs OTT Suggested Price) can be generated on request for owner review.
- **0009 Media** — **NO photography/recording/media at any time during tune appointments** (setup, flashing, teardown), incl. laptops, screens, equipment, cables, USB drives, software; and no publication of such media. ⚠ Constrains Tuned Yota marketing: never publish media of the flashing setup/equipment (see [[advertising-graphics-project]], [[dyno-proof-on-seo-pages]] — dyno charts/results OK, tune-flashing gear NOT).
- **0010 CARB → Non-CARB** — Before giving a non-CARB update to a client who has a CARB EO cal, remove or have the client surrender the CARB EO under-hood sticker. If it can't be removed/surrendered, don't provide the non-CARB service.
- **0011 Flex Fuel Tundra** — On flex-fuel-capable Tundras, MUST reset ethanol content to 0% after the INITIAL OTT calibration (once, not every change) or the learned ethanol value locks in.
- **0012 Retailer Reporting Standards (v1.2, 2/8/2026)** — the monthly report format. See the full column spec + the compliance-reconciliation status in [[monthly-ott-calibration-report]]. Key rules: Excel or Google Sheet; standard column widths/row heights; **no colors, formulas, or additional formatting** ("any deviation will break reporting"); **15 columns in exact order** ending with a **Notes** column AFTER Commission; Commission shown as **"$110.00" / "$0.00"** (with $ and 2 decimals, $0.00 if free); Vehicle Type + Engine Size + Tuning Platform + Calibration Type from fixed picklists (capitalization/spacing matter — e.g. "RAV 4" has a space, engine "2.4T"/"2.4TH"/"2.7"/"3.5"/"4.0"/"4.6"/"4.7"/"5.7", no extra zeros); Mileage no commas; ECU ID last 4-5 or full, not an "equation"; Gear Size 2-decimals or "Stock".
- **0013 Forced Induction Standards (2/25/2026)** — Before ANY supercharger/turbo/FI tuning: give OTT staff all requested vehicle/system info pre-scheduling, and the vehicle must first run the FI manufacturer's supplied "stock" calibration ≥100 miles + ≥5 heat cycles. Fix any drivability/faults first.

Non-compliance with any policy → disciplinary action up to suspension/termination as an OTT retailer.
