## AMSOIL product & program knowledge (for national-sales content / Front B)
Synthesized from our catalog + pricing sheet + the 6-competitor analysis + established AMSOIL facts. Mark (verify) = confirm before publishing as a hard claim.
- **Positioning hooks (competitor-proven):** "The First in Synthetics" (est. 1972); **factory-direct** — NOT sold at AutoZone/NAPA/O'Reilly (scarcity hook); made in the USA; superior protection + **extended drain intervals** (cost + convenience story). These are the angles every ranking competitor leans on.
- **Motor-oil tiers (use in "which AMSOIL oil" guides):** **Signature Series** (top — longest drain, ~25k mi/1yr severe-service (verify per product)), **XL** (mid, ~12k/1yr (verify)), **OE** (OEM-interval, budget). Our garage recommends **Signature Series** for the tuned Toyota/Lexus fleet.
- **Catalog breadth (for the content taxonomy):** motor oils (0W-16→20W-50; gas/diesel/European/motorcycle/marine/ATV-UTV/2-&-4-stroke/racing), **Ea filters** (oil/air/bypass), **SEVERE GEAR** gear lube (75W-85/75W-90/75W-110/75W-140/80W-90), ATF (Signature-Series **Multi-Vehicle** = ATF, **Fuel-Efficient** = ATL; OE variants; CVT/DCT), fuel additives, grease, coolant, cleaners.
- **PC = the recurring money event.** Preferred Customers buy at ~wholesale (~25% under catalog); **free AMSOIL shipping over $100**; attribution via `?zo=30713116` (30-day cookie). The compounding goal is PC (and higher tiers: Dealer, Commercial/Retail account) — a PC signup reorders for years locked to the ZO.
- **AMSOIL business ladder (each a revenue lane):** Retail buyer → **Preferred Customer** (25% off) → **Dealer** (wholesale + commission) → **Commercial / Retail / Installer accounts** (B2B, high-LTV — competitors monetize this; we don't yet).
- **Content angles that rank (from competitor gaps we can beat):** per-viscosity/fluid **product guides** ("AMSOIL 0W-20 / 5W-30 / ATF / gear-lube guide" — BuyOilDirect's 31-guide engine), **vehicle-specific** ("best AMSOIL for a tuned Tundra/Tacoma/GX" — OUR moat, done in Front A), **geo** ("Buy AMSOIL in [State]" — Synthetic Garage's engine), "AMSOIL vs conventional," "extended drain intervals," "is AMSOIL worth it."
