4Runner supercharger FAQ

The Magnuson TVS1320 supercharger for 2010–2024 4Runners is $7,695 at current Magnuson retail, with calibration included. The 2003–2009 MP90 tuner kit is $6,495 (Tuned Yota supplies the tune), and the 1996–2004 3.4L retrofit is $4,495. Installation and OTT calibration are quoted separately.

Magnuson rates the TVS1320 system at 335+ horsepower and 345+ lb-ft at the crank — 30% more power and 28% more torque than stock on 6 psi of boost. The bigger story is mid-range torque: passing, climbing and towing stop feeling like events.

Yes — 2010–2019 kits carry CARB EO D-488-45 and 2020–2024 kits carry EO D-488-64, both 50-state legal. (The 2003–2009 MP90 tuner kit is the exception: it isn't CARB-exempt and isn't sold into California.)

The 1GR-FE is one of the most boost-friendly engines Toyota makes — low compression, conservative factory tune, and a long track record of supercharged builds. The sealed Magnuson unit never needs oil changes, and Magnuson backs the system with a 3-year/36,000-mile warranty.

Yes — Magnuson makes a dedicated TVS1320 system for 2020–2024 4Runners (part 01-13-40-023-BL, $7,695, CARB EO D-488-64), and Tuned Yota ships it anywhere in the lower 48 or installs and calibrates it in-house.
