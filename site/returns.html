Tuned Yota — Return & Refund Policy

Effective July 24, 2026

Tuned Yota is an Authorized AMSOIL Dealer. When you order AMSOIL products through our site, the order is sold, billed, shipped, and fulfilled by AMSOIL Inc. on amsoil.com — we never touch your payment, and your purchase is protected by AMSOIL's own return policy. This page summarizes that policy so you know exactly where you stand, and tells you how to start a return. "Tuned Yota" is a registered assumed name (DBA) of 1st Minnesota Lending, LLC, a Minnesota limited liability company.

Contents: 30-day returns · 90-day exchanges · How to start a return · Damaged or defective items · Tuning services & hardware · Contact

AMSOIL products — 30-day returns

  • Return window: 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Condition: products must be unopened, currently marketed, and in resalable condition.
  • Refund: 100% of the purchase price plus sales tax, minus the original freight charges. Refunds go back to the original form of payment — if the order was paid by credit card, the card is reimbursed.
  • Return shipping: the customer is responsible for return freight costs.

Exchanges — 90 days

Unopened, currently marketed, resalable products may be exchanged within 90 days of purchase with no restocking fee. Exchanged products must be of equal or lesser value than those returned; exchanges do not involve cash refunds or account credits.

How to start a return

Because AMSOIL fulfills every order, returns are handled directly by AMSOIL Customer Service:

  • Phone: (800) 777-7094
  • Email: customerservice@amsoil.com
  • Online: through your account on amsoil.com

The complete, authoritative policy is published at AMSOIL's Return Policy page. If anything on this page and AMSOIL's policy ever differ, AMSOIL's policy governs.

Damaged, defective, or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or isn't what you ordered, contact AMSOIL Customer Service (above) right away — they will make it right. AMSOIL lubricants are also backed by the AMSOIL Limited Warranty covering product performance in your equipment.

Tuning services & installed hardware

Calibration installs, supercharger systems, and other in-person work are scheduled services, not mail-order products, so this return policy doesn't apply to them. For anything about your appointment, quote, or installed hardware, just reach out to us directly — we'll take care of you. See also our Terms & Conditions.

Questions? We're here

Even though AMSOIL processes the transaction, you're our customer. If you're unsure whether something is returnable, need help reaching AMSOIL, or anything else — call or text (612) 406-7117 or email info@tunedyota.com and we'll help you sort it out.
