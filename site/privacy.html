Tuned Yota — Privacy Policy

Effective July 9, 2026

This policy explains how Tuned Yota ("we," "us") handles information in the Tuned Yota mobile app and at tunedyota.com. We built the app to run our in-person tuning operations — it is deliberately lean and carries no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking.

Who uses the app

The current app is a tool for our authorized installers, who sign in with a private passcode to manage the day's tuning appointments. Customer-facing features (viewing your Certificate of Calibration, purchasing products) are planned; this policy will be updated before those launch to describe the additional information they involve.

Information the app handles

  • Installer sign-in — a private passcode and the resulting session token, used only to authenticate the installer to their own appointments.
  • Appointment & vehicle details entered by staff — such as a customer's name, phone, vehicle, VIN, mileage, and the calibration performed. This is business record information our installers enter to complete a job and issue the customer's certificate.
  • Camera (VIN scanning) — with your permission, the app uses the camera to read a vehicle's VIN barcode. Scanning happens on your device; we do not store or upload the camera images — only the resulting VIN text is saved to the appointment record.
  • Push notification token — if you allow notifications, the device's push token is stored so we can alert the installer (e.g., "your roster is ready"). It is not used for marketing.

How we use it

Solely to operate our tuning appointments: authenticate installers, schedule and complete jobs, issue the customer's Certificate of Calibration, and send operational notifications. We do not sell or rent personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Service providers

We use a small set of vendors strictly to run the service, under their own security and privacy terms:

  • Airtable — stores appointment records.
  • Netlify — hosts the app's web content and secure functions.
  • Resend — sends transactional email (e.g., certificates, confirmations).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivers push notifications.
  • Google ML Kit — performs on-device barcode scanning (no data leaves the device for this).

Security & retention

Access is restricted by installer passcodes and server-side checks; each installer sees only their own appointments. We keep appointment records as long as needed for our business and legal obligations, then delete or anonymize them.

Your choices

You can decline or revoke camera and notification permissions in your device settings at any time (the app still works — you simply type the VIN and won't receive push alerts). To ask about, correct, or delete information we hold about you, contact us below.

Children

The app is a business tool and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves (notably when customer accounts and purchasing launch). We'll revise the effective date above and, for material changes, note them in the app.
