Why AMSOIL for a tuned Toyota or Lexus

A tuned, supercharged, or towing build asks more of every fluid it runs. AMSOIL's 100% synthetic motor oils, gear lubes, and transmission fluids are built to hold their protective film under the extra heat, load, and rpm that come with more power — which is exactly why our own installers run them. As an Authorized AMSOIL Dealer, Tuned Yota lets you order the right fluids for your truck and have them shipped direct.

The AMSOIL Garage above pairs your make, model, and year with the correct grade, capacity, and severe-service drain interval — the schedule AMSOIL recommends for hard use, heat, towing, and short-trip Upper-Midwest driving. Verified per-vehicle specs are rolling out across the Toyota and Lexus lineup; the full catalog is always available to order.

New to synthetic oil, or weighing it against the factory fill? See how AMSOIL compares to Toyota and Lexus OEM fluids, or read our AMSOIL synthetic motor oil guide for how the grades, drain intervals, and warranty coverage work.
