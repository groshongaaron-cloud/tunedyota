What your kit price includes

  • Complete Magnuson supercharger system with Eaton TVS rotor assembly — the same twin-vortices technology used on the 755 hp Corvette ZR1
  • Most complete kits include Magnuson's calibration and handheld tuner — and on the 5.7L kits, a high-flow fuel pump and injectors too
  • Authorized-dealer sourcing: Magnuson's 3-year/36,000-mile system warranty, with a limited powertrain warranty on eligible vehicles at no extra cost
  • Drop-ship anywhere in the lower 48, or hand delivery to your Tuned Yota install appointment

Quoted separately

  • Installation — most builds are installed at our Upper Midwest events (MN, IA, WI, ND, SD, NE)
  • OTT calibration — the tune that makes the blower behave like factory equipment
  • Supporting accessories where applicable (heat exchanger hardware, colored lid on Tacoma +$300)

Emissions legality (CARB)

Almost every Magnuson Toyota system is CARB 50-state legal with an Executive Order on file: Tundra / Land Cruiser / LX570 / Sequoia 5.7L TVS2650 — EO D-488-69 · Tacoma 3.5L TVS1900 — EO D-488-59 · Tacoma/FJ 4.0L MP90 — EO D-488-40 · 4Runner TVS1320 — EO D-488-45 (2010–19) and D-488-64 (2020–24) · 3.4L 5VZ retrofit — EO D-488-57 · Land Cruiser 80-Series Classic — EO D-488-71. Exceptions: the 4Runner MP90 tuner kit is not CARB-exempt, and the 2022+ Performance Packs are 49-state (California EO pending).
