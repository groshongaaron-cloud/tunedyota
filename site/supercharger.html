Why add a supercharger

  • Big, usable power and torque across the whole rev range, not just a peak number
  • Real towing and overland confidence under load, lift, armor, and larger tires
  • OEM-smooth, daily-drivable power delivery when paired with a proper calibration
  • Intercooled positive-displacement systems engineered for the platform
  • Backed by the calibrator with more forced-induction Toyota experience than anyone

How a supercharged build works

1

Choose your system

We'll match your vehicle to the right Magnuson kit (or a supported Harrop or ProCharger system). Factory-turbo platforms instead get a dedicated turbo performance calibration.

2

Get the hardware

As a Magnuson dealer we drop-ship kits anywhere in the lower 48, or install yours in-house if you're in the Upper Midwest.

3

Calibrate it

The supercharger is dialed in with a calibration built for the system by a licensed VFTuner PRO Tuner, custom calibrations are refined over multiple log reviews.

4

Verify & drive

Every calibration is verified with a 5-gas analyzer, emissions fully intact and EPA-compliant in every state, no check-engine-light defeat, no off-road-only tuning.
