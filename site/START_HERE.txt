TUNED YOTA — FRESH WEBSITE
==========================
This folder is your complete website. Every page is here and already linked
together (Home, Find Your Tune, FAQ, OTT, Team, and all 13 vehicle pages).
"index.html" is your home page.

HOW TO PUT IT LIVE (easiest method — no coding):
1. Go to  app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag this ENTIRE folder onto that page.
3. Wait ~30 seconds. You get a live web address you can open and share.
   (This is a private preview — it is NOT your real domain yet.)
4. Click around and test everything on your phone and computer.

CONNECT YOUR DOMAIN (when you're happy):
5. In Netlify: Site settings > Domain management > Add a custom domain >
   enter  tunedyota.com  and follow the DNS steps it shows you.
   NOTE: pointing the domain here REPLACES the current Wix site at that address.
   Do this only when you're ready to switch over.

THINGS TO KNOW:
- The team/installer photos are bundled in the "images" folder, so the
  site no longer depends on Wix for anything. Keep that folder with the rest
  of the files when you upload.
- To preview locally first, just double-click index.html (most things work;
  the map and YouTube load best on the live Netlify address).
