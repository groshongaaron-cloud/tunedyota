What affects the price?

Your price depends on your platform, model year, and engine, and on the calibration path you choose: the base OTT Tune, a custom calibration with multiple log reviews, or a forced-induction (supercharger / factory-turbo) calibration for the biggest gains.

What's included

  • A custom calibration built by a licensed VFTuner PRO Tuner
  • Factory emissions kept fully intact, verified with a 5-gas analyzer
  • In-person tuning at a scheduled event in your region
  • Free updates for an adjustment, or after a lift, larger tires, gears, or steel bumpers
