What Is the OTT Tune?

An OTT Tune (Overland Tailor Tune) is a custom ECU and transmission calibration for Toyota and Lexus vehicles that eliminates gear hunting, sharpens throttle response, and restores power lost to big tires and heavy loads. It's installed in person by authorized installers and costs $400–$550 for most platforms. Tuned Yota is an authorized OTT installer for the Upper Midwest, calibrating your vehicle in person at events across Minnesota, Iowa, Wisconsin, North Dakota, South Dakota, and Nebraska.

OTT vs. a stock or generic tune

The factory calibration is a compromise, tuned for emissions cycles, global fuel, and the mildest possible owner, not for your tires, your load, or how you actually drive. A generic "canned" flash swings the other way and ignores your exact platform and modifications. OTT sits where you want to be: a platform-specific Overland Tailor calibration, dialed in person to your year, engine, tire size, and setup, with your factory emissions systems left fully intact.
