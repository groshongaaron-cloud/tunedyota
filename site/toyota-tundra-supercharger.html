What the TVS2650 does for the 5.7L

  • 560 hp and 563 lb-ft at the crank — a 47% horsepower jump on just 7 psi, from Eaton's TVS2650 rotor pack, the same twin-vortices architecture behind the 755 hp Corvette ZR1
  • On our own dyno, supercharged 5.7L Tundras have gone from roughly 292 to ~450 wheel horsepower with install and OTT calibration
  • Truck-shaped power: instant, low-RPM boost with no turbo lag — exactly where towing and passing happen
  • Everything's in the box: dual air-to-water charge coolers, high-flow fuel pump and injectors, and the handheld tuner with Magnuson's calibration
  • CARB 50-state legal (EO D-488-69) and backed by Magnuson's 3-year/36,000-mile system warranty
  • Flex-fuel trucks get a dedicated system, and the FFV HP Tuners RTD upgrade ($1,299) adds a device, calibration and 8 high-flow injectors for serious E85 power

Already running a TRD or TVS1900 blower?

Magnuson's $5,195 Magnum upgrade system swaps your existing TVS1900-based blower (TRD or Magnuson) to the full TVS2650 — reusing your truck's front drive, cooling system and existing tune. It's the 560 hp rotor pack without paying for the whole kit twice. Tuned Yota can verify what's on your truck and handle the changeover.
