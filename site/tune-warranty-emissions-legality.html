Does a tune void my factory warranty?

A tune does not automatically void your warranty. Under the federal Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, a manufacturer must show that a modification actually caused a failure before it can deny that specific claim. Because Tuned Yota keeps your factory emissions systems fully intact, the calibration targets drivability, not defeating any emissions protection. If a warranty claim is ever questioned, the burden is on the manufacturer to prove the modification caused the failure.

Is the OTT Tune emissions-legal?

Yes. Your factory emissions systems stay fully intact and every calibration is verified with a 5-gas analyzer during drive-cycle testing, EPA-compliant in every state. Emissions requirements vary by state, so contact us to confirm what applies to your vehicle.

Will it throw a check-engine light? Do you tune deletes?

No on both. Tuned Yota never disables check-engine lights, and does not tune off-road-only vehicles or remove emissions equipment. All vehicles must retain fully intact, federally compliant emissions systems.
