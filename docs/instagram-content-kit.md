## A) 10 ready-to-shoot Reel scripts
Format for each: **Hook (0–2s)** → **Shots** → **On-screen text** → **Caption** → **CTA**.
Shoot vertical (9:16), good light, sound on, always add captions.

**1． 3 Signs Your Tacoma Needs a Tune**
- Hook: "Your Tacoma is trying to tell you something."
- Shots: truck driving, tach hunting, downshift clunk, hand on shifter.
- On-screen: "1) Gear hunting 2) Lazy throttle 3) Worse after 35s tires"
- Caption: Common on the Tacoma after tires/weight — calibration fixes shift logic + throttle, emissions fully intact.
- CTA: "Find your exact tune → link in bio."

**2． Why Your 4Runner Hunts Gears (and the fix)**
- Hook: "If your 4Runner keeps shifting on hills — watch this."
- Shots: tach bouncing between gears uphill, then smooth after.
- On-screen: "Stock trans logic wasn't built for your build."
- Caption: A calibration retunes shift points so it stops chasing gears. 5-gas verified, CEL stays off.
- CTA: "DM your year + engine."

**3． Dyno Pull (sound on)**
- Hook: "Turn your sound on. 🔊"
- Shots: strap-down, pull, numbers climbing, reaction.
- On-screen: "OTT calibration — real-world power."
- Caption: We tune for usable power + drivability, not just peak numbers.
- CTA: "Find your tune → link in bio."

**4． Before / After Throttle Response**
- Hook: "Same truck. Same pedal. Watch the difference."
- Shots: split-screen pedal-to-response before vs after.
- On-screen: "Before ❌ lag  /  After ✅ instant"
- Caption: Sharper throttle is the #1 thing people feel day one.
- CTA: "Book yours — link in bio."

**5． Magnuson Supercharger Install (timelapse)**
- Hook: "From stock to supercharged. ⏱️"
- Shots: timelapse of the install, beauty shot of the finished engine bay.
- On-screen: "Authorized Magnuson dealer + calibration"
- Caption: We sell, install, AND calibrate Magnuson — or drop-ship anywhere in the lower 48.
- CTA: "Supercharger options → link in bio."

**6． "Emissions Intact" — the 5-gas proof**
- Hook: "Tuned doesn't mean dirty."
- Shots: 5-gas analyzer probe + readout.
- On-screen: "Factory emissions intact · EPA-compliant · no CEL defeat"
- Caption: Every calibration is 5-gas verified. Street-legal, done right.
- CTA: "Questions? FAQ in bio."

**7． Customer First-Drive Reaction**
- Hook: "First drive after the tune. 😮"
- Shots: customer driving, genuine reaction, quick testimonial line.
- On-screen: customer name + vehicle + city.
- Caption: Nothing beats the first drive. [City], you're next.
- CTA: "Find your tune → link in bio."

**8． What Is the OTT Tune? (60-sec explainer)**
- Hook: "OTT tune — what it actually does."
- Shots: talking-head + b-roll of driving/shifting.
- On-screen: "Drivability • shifting • throttle • towing • usable power"
- Caption: OTT builds the calibration, Tuned Yota installs + dials it in. Built by a licensed VFTuner PRO Tuner.
- CTA: "Learn more → link in bio."

**9． Tundra Towing Transformation**
- Hook: "Towing with your Tundra shouldn't feel like this."
- Shots: loaded trailer, downshift hunting up a grade, then steady after.
- On-screen: "Steady power + shift logic under load."
- Caption: Calibration makes towing calmer and more confident.
- CTA: "Find your tune → link in bio."

**10． Find Your Exact Tune in 60 Seconds (screen-record)**
- Hook: "Get your price in under a minute."

## B) 30-day content calendar
~5 posts/week (mostly Reels), balanced across pillars. Stories daily on top of this.
R = Reel, C = Carousel, P = Photo, S = Story-only day.

**Week 1**
- Mon (R): Reel #1 — 3 signs your Tacoma needs a tune
- Tue (C): Vehicles we tune + "from $X" → link in bio
- Wed (R): Reel #3 — dyno pull
- Thu (P): Clean customer rig + short testimonial
- Fri (R): Reel #6 — 5-gas "emissions intact"
- Sat (S): Poll — "What bugs you most about how your rig drives?"

**Week 2**
- Mon (R): Reel #2 — why your 4Runner hunts gears
- Tue (P): Team spotlight (Aaron/Noah/Cody) + service area
- Wed (R): Reel #5 — supercharger install timelapse
- Thu (C): "OTT vs stock" before/after points
- Fri (R): Reel #4 — before/after throttle
- Sat (S): Q&A "Ask a tuner"

**Week 3**
- Mon (R): Reel #9 — Tundra towing transformation
- Tue (P): Event announcement / where we'll be next (ties to event map)
- Wed (R): Reel #8 — what is the OTT tune
- Thu (C): Supercharger-ready vehicles + "ships lower 48"
- Fri (R): Reel #7 — customer first-drive reaction
- Sat (S): Behind the scenes at an install

**Week 4**
- Mon (R): Reel #10 — find your exact tune in 60 sec
- Tue (P): Review screenshot (with permission) + thank you
- Wed (R): New angle — "3 signs your 4Runner/Tundra needs a tune"
- Thu (C): FAQ carousel (cost, emissions, who installs, booking)
- Fri (R): Best-performing Reel remix / new build feature
- Sat (S): Recap + CTA "Find your tune before [next event]"

Repeat monthly; swap topics by season (towing in summer, cold-start drivability in winter).
