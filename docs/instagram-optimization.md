## 3. Link in bio (drive conversions + feed the pixel)
- Point the bio link to your highest-intent page:
  `https://tunedyota.com/find-your-exact-tune?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio`
- The UTM lets you see IG traffic in analytics, and every click now hits the **Meta Pixel**
  → you can **retarget IG visitors** with ads.
- Optional: a small link hub (your own page or Linktree) with: Find Your Tune · Call/Text ·
  Superchargers · FAQ. (Direct-to-tune-finder usually converts best.)

## 4. Content pillars (rotate these)
1. **Proof/results** — before/after shift feel, dyno pulls, 5-gas "emissions intact."
2. **Education** — "why your 4Runner hunts gears," "supercharger 101," "what OTT does."
3. **Eye candy/builds** — clean trucks, installs, details.
4. **Social proof** — reviews, customer rigs, testimonials.
5. **Behind the scenes / events** — team, install days, "where we'll be next" (ties to your
   event map).
6. **Offers/CTA** — find your tune, book, supercharger drop-ship anywhere in the lower 48.

## 5. Reels-first (most important for reach)
Post **3–5x/week, mostly Reels** (short vertical video). High-performing ideas:
- "3 signs your Tacoma needs a tune"
- Dyno pull (sound on)
- Before/after throttle response, side by side
- Supercharger install timelapse
- 5-gas analyzer proof ("emissions stay intact")
- Customer first-drive reaction
- "Why your [model] hunts gears — and the fix"
Hook in the first 1–2 seconds; add captions (most watch muted); end with a CTA.

## 8. Local discovery
- Geotag posts/Reels with the event city; use the location tag.
- Tag customers (with permission) and local shops/clubs; collaborate on Reels.
- Use local hashtags so you show up for "near me" browsing.

## 9. Engagement routine
- Stories **daily** (polls, Q&A "ask a tuner," event countdowns).
- Reply to every comment/DM quickly (DMs are leads).
- Spend 10 min/day engaging on Toyota/Lexus community accounts + your local tags.

## 10. Connect it to the engine we built
- Bio link → tune finder (UTM) → **Meta Pixel fires** → retarget IG visitors with ads.
- Cross-post Reels as Facebook videos; funnel followers into your Midwest Tuning Group.
- Use IG Insights to double down on whatever pillar/Reel format performs best.

### Honest note
Growth compounds with **consistency + Reels + replying fast**. One great month then silence
won't move it. Pick a realistic cadence you can sustain (even 3 Reels/week beats sporadic
bursts).
