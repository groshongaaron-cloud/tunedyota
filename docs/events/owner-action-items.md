Just tell Claude the address for an event — e.g.
**"Set the Milwaukee Aug 22 venue to `Shop Name, 123 Main St, Milwaukee, WI 53202`."**

reminder emails + the installer roster, runs the tests, ships it, and confirms live.
Prefer not to type it in chat? Copy the address to your clipboard and say "clipboard."

> **Why it matters:** until a venue is set, customer reminder emails (sent 10 / 2 / 0 days
> out) and the installer roster show **"Address: To Be Released"** for that event.
