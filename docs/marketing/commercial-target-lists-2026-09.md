- **Order:** start with your geographically densest cluster (Aaron: Apple
  Valley/Lakeville — several are minutes from the shop; Noah: Fox Valley;
  Cody: Sioux Falls) so Touch-1 mornings hit 3-4 doors.
- **Pace:** 5 Touch-1 visits/week per territory = every list worked by
  early October.
- **Tracking:** one `commercial` lead per business BEFORE the first visit;
  stage notes per touch; audits delivered + accounts opened tallied monthly.
- Big-fleet priority targets (most account revenue per close): Weller Brothers
  (×2 metros), H&H Lawn and Landscape, Specialized Property Care, Crawford
