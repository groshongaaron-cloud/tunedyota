## 1. What this marketing is actually for

Tuned Yota makes money when a Toyota/Lexus owner **books a slot at an event** (or a
supercharger job). Every channel below exists to feed that one outcome. Four goals, in order:

1. **Fill event slots** — drive booked appointments at each 2026/2027 event city.
2. **Build authority** — be the obvious, trusted Toyota/Lexus tuning expert in the Upper Midwest (and citable by AI).
3. **Grow an owned audience** — followers, the Facebook group, email/SMS list — so each new event launches to a warm crowd, not a cold ad spend.
4. **Generate social proof** — reviews, customer builds, dyno results — which compound every other goal.

**The unfair advantage we now have:** real, measured **dyno proof** is live on the site
(Magnuson guide + Tundra/Tacoma/4Runner pages — 450/362/334 whp supercharged, real NA
Stock-vs-OTT gains). Proof out-converts claims. The entire content engine should lean on it.

## 4. Content pillars & the repurposing engine

**Six content pillars** (unified across channels): **Proof/results · Education · Builds/eye-candy ·
Social proof · Events/behind-the-scenes · Offers/CTA.** (Matches the IG pillars; now applied everywhere.)

**Shoot once, cut everywhere.** The core discipline that makes multi-channel sustainable:
- **One event day or install** → a long-form YouTube video + 5–8 vertical clips (Reels/Shorts/TikTok) + photos (feed/carousels) + a testimonial + a dyno card.
- **One dyno pull** → a sound-on Reel/Short/TikTok + a dyno card graphic + a feed post + an ad creative.
- Capture vertical 9:16, sound on, good light, always caption (most watch muted).

## 5. Per-channel playbooks

### Website (tunedyota.com) — the conversion hub
Already strong (SEO/AEO pages, dyno proof, Find Your Exact Tune, Meta Pixel, funnel tracking).
Marketing job: **every channel's link points here with a UTM**, and the site converts.
- Primary destination: `find-your-exact-tune?utm_source=<channel>&utm_medium=<type>&utm_campaign=<event-or-topic>`.
- Keep the dyno proof and event dates current; add new vehicle/dyno pages as assets arrive.

### Instagram (@tunedyota) — top of the organic funnel
→ Full detail in `instagram-content-kit.md` + `instagram-optimization.md`. Reels-first,
3–5×/week, Stories daily, bio link with UTM, geotag event cities. **Add:** every dyno card
and event flyer from §3.

### Facebook (Page + Midwest Tuning Group + Meta Ads)
→ Full detail in `lead-generation-playbook.md`. Three roles:
- **Page:** cross-post IG Reels as FB video; event announcements; reviews.
- **Midwest Tuning Group (owned audience):** value-first posts 3–4×/wk, recurring "what bugs you about how it drives?" threads, funnel into events. This is the highest-trust owned channel.
- **Meta Ads:** the paid workhorse (§7) — Pixel is live, so retarget site/IG/video audiences.

### YouTube — authority + evergreen search (NEW)
The platform where proof-heavy content ranks for years and AI/Google surface it.
- **Long-form (1–2/month):** "Magnuson supercharger install + dyno on a 5.7 Tundra (292→450 whp)", "What the OTT tune actually does (with dyno)", "Stock vs OTT vs Supercharged — 4Runner", "Is a Toyota tune worth it?" Mirror the site's high-intent pages → cross-link both ways.
- **Shorts (2–4/week):** the same vertical clips cut for Reels/TikTok. Free reach + funnels to long-form.
- **Optimize:** keyword titles ("Toyota Tundra dyno"), 1280×720 thumbnails (§3), description links to Find Your Exact Tune (UTM), playlists per platform (Tundra/Tacoma/4Runner/Supercharger).
- **Why it adds value:** YouTube is the #2 search engine; a "Tundra supercharger dyno" video is an asset that sells for years and feeds the AEO strategy.

### TikTok (@tunedyota) — reach + younger/newer audience (NEW)
- Post the same vertical clips (3–5/week): dyno pulls (sound on), "3 signs your [truck] needs a tune", install timelapses, first-drive reactions, "Truth Be Told" myth-busting.
- Native captions + trending audio where it fits the brand; hook in 1 sec.
- Link in bio → Find Your Exact Tune (UTM). Tag event cities.
- **Why it adds value:** highest organic reach-per-effort right now; truck/overland content performs. Low marginal cost since clips are already shot for Reels/Shorts.

### Google Business Profile — local "near me" keystone
→ `docs/seo/gbp-setup.md`. Critical for geo queries (the diagnosis: we rank ~#2 in WI mainly
because no GBP). Post events + photos there; collect Google reviews (§6). **Owner-executed.**

### Email / SMS — the lifecycle channel (owned, highest ROI)
We already capture booking emails/phones in Airtable and send event reminders. Extend into marketing:
- **Pre-event:** "event coming to [city] — book before slots fill" to that region's list.
- **Post-event:** thank-you + **review request** (§6) + "refer a friend."
- **Nurture:** seasonal ("winter cold-start drivability", "towing season"), new dyno results, supercharger offers.
- Keep it permission-based and useful. SMS for time-sensitive event/roster nudges only.

### Reddit / Forums (TacomaWorld, IH8MUD, Tundras.com) — intent capture
→ `lead-generation-playbook.md` lead-radar agent already scans these. Engage value-first per
the templates; never scrape, never spam. Authority play, not volume.

### Optional / later
- **Pinterest:** build/spec pins → evergreen search traffic (low effort if repurposing graphics).
- **YouTube/Google Ads:** once long-form library exists, run in-stream + search ads on high-intent terms.

## 7. Paid media plan
Start lean, let proof do the work, scale winners.
- **Meta (lead workhorse):** §1 of the lead-gen playbook — $10–20/day to start, 2–3 ad sets, traffic/leads → Find Your Exact Tune. Best creative: **dyno proof video + bold hook.** Retarget site/IG/video viewers + group members (Pixel live). Geo-ring each event city ~4–6 weeks out.
- **TikTok ads (test):** spark-ad your best-performing organic clips to in-market truck audiences once organic shows a winner.
- **YouTube/Google ads (later):** in-stream + search once the long-form library exists.
- **Always:** UTM every link; one campaign per event city + one always-on awareness campaign.

Every event city in `docs/events/2026-2027-event-plan.md` runs the same lifecycle. This is

| Timing | Action | Assets |
|---|---|---|

This ties marketing directly to the roster-fill goal and reuses the same calendar the

## 9. Measurement & KPIs
Use what's already built: **UTMs → Meta Pixel → Funnel Events → `aggregateFunnel`** (the
monthly `submissions-report` already summarizes funnel drop-off).
- **North-star:** booked event slots per city (and supercharger inquiries).

- **Leading indicators:** follower growth, Reel/Short/TikTok reach, group members, Google reviews, email list size.
- **Cadence:** monthly review (fold into the existing submissions-report). Double down on the pillar/channel/creative that converts; cut what doesn't.

## 10. Production workflow & ownership
- **Capture:** at every event/install, one person shoots vertical clips + photos + one testimonial (a phone is enough). This is the raw material for the whole month.
- **Asset library:** keep working/source files in `assets-source/` (gitignored, off the public site) — the `Advertisement Ideas/` drafts live here. Only curated, web-ready images go into `site/images/`.
- **Templates:** Canva brand kit (owner-editable) + HTML generators for data cards (dyno/pricing).
- **Cadence floor (sustainable beats heroic):** 3 Reels/Shorts/TikToks per week, 1 YouTube long-form per month, Stories daily, 3–4 group posts/week, 1 review ask + 1 testimonial per event. Consistency compounds; bursts don't.

## 12. Guardrails
- **Honesty on claims:** dyno numbers always "results vary by build/fuel/mods"; emissions language stays accurate and consistent with the warranty page; never imply CARB-legal where unconfirmed.
- **Permission:** get customer OK before posting their rig/face/review.
- **Platform rules:** no scraping, no spam, disclose we're a tuner in communities (per the lead-gen playbook).
- **Brand consistency:** every asset uses the §3 tokens, the chevron mark, and "Undeniable Performance."
