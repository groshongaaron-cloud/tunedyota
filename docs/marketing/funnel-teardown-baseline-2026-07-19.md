## Executive summary

Tuned Yota sells a trust-heavy, restorative performance product — the OTT Tune —
positioned not as a risky mod but as an emissions-legal *fix* for factory drivability
flaws, installed in person and 5-gas verified. Wide SEO/authority front-end (per-vehicle
and per-state pages) funnels into one conversion tool ("Find Your Exact Tune"), with a
high-ticket supercharger arm and a recurring AMSOIL annuity behind it. The engine is the
$400–$550 event tune — high volume, near-pure-labor margin, and the relationship that
seeds every other sale. Biggest leaks are conversion-side: almost no social proof or
scarcity on the booking page, no nurture for not-ready traffic, and under-monetized
post-tune moments (AMSOIL, referrals, financing).

## 1. Core offer & positioning
OTT Tune calibration for Toyota & Lexus. Restorative/defensive framing ("ends gear
hunting, sharpens throttle, unlocks real usable power"; "even bone-stock vehicles
benefit"). Wrapped in authority + legality (5-gas verified, EPA-compliant, since 2008).
Wedge: "in-person calibration at events… never a blind, mailed-in flash."

## 2. Ideal customer profile
Upper-Midwest Toyota/Lexus truck/SUV owners (Tacoma, Tundra, 4Runner, Land Cruiser,
Sequoia, FJ, Lexus GX/LX/RX). Reliability- and legality-conscious (not the delete crowd);
overland/tow/daily-driver. Triggers: daily gear-hunting/throttle-lag frustration; recent
mods (tires/lift/armor) hurting drivability; wanting usable low-end torque for towing.

## 3. Full catalog / offers
- OTT Tune $400–$550 (K-Line 2003–04 $400; 2024+ turbo higher; custom higher).
- Free OTT Update (re-engagement).
- Magnuson superchargers $4,495–$8,395 (install + cal separate).
- 2022+ Tundra/Sequoia Performance Packs $1,500–$3,846.
- AMSOIL retail + Preferred Customer (recurring) + Dealer recruitment.
- Client account portal (certificates, My Garage).

## 4. Lead magnets & entry points
"Find Your Exact Tune" tool (primary); Free OTT Update; Priority Wait List; free
supercharger reservation; ~13 per-vehicle + 6 per-state SEO pages; AMSOIL garage; Facebook
/ Midwest Tuning Group. GAP: no soft opt-in for not-ready researchers.

## 5. Funnel structure
SEO/social → homepage or model/state page → Find Your Exact Tune (platform→model→
year/engine→priorities→calibratable?→market→lead form) → submit → booking OR Priority Wait
List → installer confirms by call/text/email → in-person event calibration → Certificate of
Calibration + account link → AMSOIL PC + free updates + supercharger upsell.

## 6. Messaging pillars
Fix-the-factory-flaws · legality & safety (5-gas, EPA, no MIL defeat) · in-person authority
· usable low-end torque · local/regional trust · category authority (OTT = most FI Toyota
calibrations).

## 7. Pricing strategy
Transparent tiered starting prices as anchors; "suggested starting prices, final quote at
booking" preserves up-quote room. Free update + free reservation reduce friction.
Supercharger high anchor makes tune feel cheap. Performance Pack ladder = productized
mid-ticket. AMSOIL = recurring. NO financing anywhere.

## 8. Unique mechanism
"In-person, 5-gas-verified, emissions-intact calibration by a licensed VFTuner PRO at a
local event — not a blind mailed flash." The 5-gas verification is the ownable proof point
vs. mail-order and vs. OTT's own remote network. Underplayed today.

## 9. Strongest CTAs
"Get OTT Now! →", "Find Your Exact Tune" (4× on model pages), "Book Event Time Slot NOW",
"Schedule my FREE OTT Update", "Book at an Event →", "Reserve this kit", + site-wide
"Call or text (612) 406-7117" (high-touch human close).

## 10. Where the money likely is
1) $400–$550 event tune = volume/margin engine + relationship seed. 2) 2022+ Tundra
Performance Packs = best margin-per-effort. 3) Superchargers = biggest ticket but kit is
COGS; profit is install+cal labor. 4) AMSOIL PC = compounding recurring. Under-harvested:
pack ladder, supercharger cal labor, AMSOIL recurring.

## 11. Biggest weaknesses / missed opportunities
1. No social proof on the conversion page; 94% FB stat not surfaced as a badge.
2. No scarcity on an inherently scarce (event-slot) offer — copy even says "no pressure."
3. No nurture path for not-ready SEO traffic (only demand-based Wait List).
4. No financing on $1,500–$8,395 offers.
5. AMSOIL recurring under-pushed at peak satisfaction (post-tune).
6. No referral loop despite an active community.

## 12. Highest-leverage moves (own funnel)
1. Proof on the booking page (94% badge + model-tagged reviews next to CTA).
2. Live event-slot scarcity ("3 of 12 left — Aug 29, Twin Cities") + next-date countdown.
3. Researcher on-ramp (soft opt-in + short nurture) + one-tap AMSOIL PC enroll & referral
   offer at certificate/close.

## Prioritized shortlist

close · 4) Financing on packs/superchargers · 5) Researcher lead magnet + nurture ·
