Ready-to-paste content to make Tuned Yota show up when people search **"AMSOIL dealer near me,"
"AMSOIL for my Tundra,"** etc. This layers the AMSOIL angle onto the general GBP setup in
[`local-seo-checklist.md`](../local-seo-checklist.md) §2 — do that first (claim + verify the
profile as a Service-Area Business), then paste the sections below.

**Referral is baked into every order link here** (`zo=30713116`) — a customer who taps through
and buys is attributed to Tuned Yota for 30 days. Prices are current as of the site catalog;
they refresh weekly, so treat them as "from $X" and let the live page show the exact number.

---

## 1. Categories — the honest call

GBP categories are **location/storefront** oriented. Tuned Yota sells AMSOIL **online, shipped
direct** (no parts counter), so do **NOT** add "Auto parts store" or "Oil change service" —
those imply a physical retail/service location and can trigger a suspension for a Service-Area
Business. Keep the tuning primary category from the main checklist. **Express the AMSOIL offering
through Services, Products, Posts, and the description below** — that's where it belongs and where
it actually helps you rank for AMSOIL queries.

- **Keep primary:** Auto tuning service (or Auto repair shop).
- **Optional secondary (only if accurate):** "Synthetic lubricant supplier" or "Distributor" — add
  only if Google offers it and you're comfortable it fits a ships-direct dealer. When unsure, skip.
