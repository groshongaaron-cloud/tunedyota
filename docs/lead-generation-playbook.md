- Detailed interests: Toyota Tacoma, Toyota 4Runner, Toyota Tundra, Land Cruiser, FJ
  Cruiser, Lexus GX, overlanding, off-roading, vehicle modification, car tuning, ECU

**Creative (test 3)**

**Copy angles (hooks)**

**Budget:** start $10–20/day, 2–3 ad sets, run 5–7 days, kill losers, scale winners.

## 3. Engagement playbook — etiquette + templates

**Rules of the road**
- Lead with genuine help; disclose you're a tuner (Tuned Yota). Never hard-sell.
- Respect group rules — many ban solicitation. Comment helpfully in public; only go to DM
  if the person invites it.
- Be the trusted expert, not the salesman. Reputation compounds.

**Template — someone asks about gear hunting / shifting**
> "That gear hunting is super common on the [model] after larger tires/added weight — the
> trans logic is calibrated for stock. A calibration retunes shift points and throttle so
> it stops chasing gears. Happy to explain what's involved if useful — no pressure."

**Template — someone asks "anyone tuned a [Tundra/4Runner]?"**
> "Yeah, [model] responds really well — mostly drivability: smoother shifts, sharper
> throttle, better towing, usable power where the platform supports it. We keep emissions
> fully intact (5-gas verified). Glad to share specifics for your year/engine."

**Template — forced induction / supercharger interest**
> "If you want a bigger jump, a Magnuson supercharger + calibration is the move on the
> [model]. We're an authorized Magnuson dealer and can drop-ship in the lower 48 or
> install + tune in-house. Want the supported options for your truck?"

**Template — soft DM follow-up (only if invited)**
> "Thanks for reaching out! Quickest way to see options + a starting price for your exact
> year/engine is our Find Your Exact Tune tool: https://tunedyota.com/find-your-exact-tune
> — or just text me the details at (612) 406-7117."
