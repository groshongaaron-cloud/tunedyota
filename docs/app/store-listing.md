## Full description
> Tuned Yota is the field companion for our authorized Toyota & Lexus tuning installers.
>
> • See your event roster and today's appointments at a glance
> • Scan a vehicle's VIN with your camera — no more typing 17 characters
> • Close out each job (calibration, mileage, details) and send the customer's Certificate of Calibration on the spot
> • Add walk-ins in seconds
> • Face ID / passcode lock and push alerts when your roster is ready
>
> This app is for Tuned Yota installers and requires an installer passcode.
